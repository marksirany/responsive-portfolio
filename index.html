About Me

I am starting a new career as a Developer after over 10 years as a technical writer.

I have worked with senior engineers, service technicians, product managers, and marketing teams to write owner’s manuals, service manuals, installation manuals, and product guides for irrigation machinery, machine control panels, control panel software and hardware, network connectivity, and communication systems.

I also designed, managed, and updated Valmont Industrie’s Irrigation Division international product documentation website and managed the content database for the domestic product documentation website. This required continuous translation management and updating into 16 languages.

I have a Bachelor of Arts Degree in English specializing in American Literature and Creative Writing from the University of Nebraska at Omaha.
